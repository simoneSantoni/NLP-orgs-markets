The following step consists of identifying the ten vectors that are most associated with each individual target words. To do that, we take advantage of the function â€œmost_similarâ€ included in the Gensim library. The below-display nested for loop iterates over the elements of the list â€œplayersâ€ (step 1) and over the neighbor positions (step 2) to populate two containers: â€œword_clustersâ€ has the neighbor words, while â€œembedding_clustersâ€ the neighbor wordsâ€™ embeddings.
